SIGNATURE DISH


'Hot Butter Cuttlefish!'

Spicy Corner an authentic Sri Lankan flavoured restaurant draws its uniqueness from the natural fresh Seafood. The most noble and natural pick as Signature dish is none other than the scrumptious mouthwatering, ‘Hot Butter Cuttlefish’!

Freshly caught succulent, tender Cuttlefish is cleaned cut into desired shape and marinated with exclusive Sri Lankan authentic spices first; then dipped in batter seasoned with local herbs; shallow fried in hot, creamy, sizzling butter till it is crispy and golden brown in colour. It is then served hot with a dip of mayonnaise or ketchup as desired. The melting of the golden, crispy stripes of cuttlefish in the mouth is an exhilarating experience that will tantalize your taste buds.
